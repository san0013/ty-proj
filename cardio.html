Cardio

Cardio is a type of physical activity that improves cardiovascular health.

  • Running: Great for building endurance and burning calories.
  • Swimming: A full-body workout that is easy on the joints.
  • Cycling: Excellent for leg strength and cardiovascular fitness.
  • Jump Rope: A high-intensity workout that improves coordination.
  • Rowing: A low-impact exercise that works multiple muscle groups.
  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training): Short bursts of intense exercise followed by rest.
  • Food Options for Running and Cardio
  • Pre-Workout Snacks: Banana: Quick source of energy. Oatmeal with Honey: Provides sustained energy for longer workouts. Peanut Butter Sandwich: Whole grain bread with peanut butter for protein and carbs.
  • Post-Workout Meals: Protein Smoothie: Made with yogurt, fruits, and a scoop of protein powder. Vegetable Dosa with Sambar: A nutritious meal with carbs and protein. Chickpea Salad: High in protein and fiber, great for recovery.
